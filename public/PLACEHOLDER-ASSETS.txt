Production setup checklist:
  1. Set ADMIN_EMAIL in .env (matches your Google sign-in email)
  2. Run: npx prisma migrate deploy
  3. Sign in once as admin to activate ADMIN role
  4. Optional: UPSTASH_REDIS_REST_URL + UPSTASH_REDIS_REST_TOKEN for production rate limits
  5. Replace placeholder YouTube URLs in src/data/artworks.json with your real videos
  6. Update socialLinks in src/data/config.json with your live profile URLs
